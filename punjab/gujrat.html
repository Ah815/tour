GUJRAT

Gujrat (Punjabi and Urdu: گُجرات) is a city in the Punjab Province of Pakistan.[5] It is the capital of Gujrat District and it is the 21st largest city of Pakistan by population.[6][4] Along with the nearby cities of Sialkot and Gujranwala, Gujrat forms part of the Golden Triangle of industrial cities with export-oriented economies.
